Events

Plant sales, fundraisers, workdays, holiday lights, and more. Check here before visiting on weekends — we sometimes close for private events.

🔴 Park closure notice: When an event lists "Park closed to general visitors," the park will be closed to drop-in guests that day. We recommend checking this page before weekend visits.

🌿 Nursery Schedule

Our Hidden Garden Nursery sells volunteer-propagated plants. Open Tuesdays and Saturdays, 9am–Noon, October through May.

More about the nursery →

📷 Art in the Park

A different local artist fills the Galleria each month. New artist receptions are usually the last Sunday of the month, 4–7pm.

🎄 Winter Lights

Our biggest annual event — acres of holiday lights, food trucks, Santa, and live music. Happens each December. Over 8,500 visitors last year.

Rent the Park

Weddings, parties, and private events are how we keep the lights on. We close to the public when a rental is in progress.
