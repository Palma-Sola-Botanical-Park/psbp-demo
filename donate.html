Keep it free. Keep it open.

We receive no government funding. No entrance fee. Your donation is what makes both of those things possible — and has been since 1990.

Securities and mutual funds held more than one year can be donated to the park, potentially eliminating federal capital gains taxes on the transfer. Contact your financial advisor for specifics, and reach out to us to coordinate the transfer.

In-Kind Donations

Tools, gardening supplies, baked goods for events, professional services. We're a scrappy operation and genuinely use all of it. Contact us to discuss.

Palma Sola Botanical Park Foundation, Inc. is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible in accordance with current IRS code. You will receive a receipt for your records.
