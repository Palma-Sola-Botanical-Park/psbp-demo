How this works: All three themes share the same pages, events list, and iNaturalist feed. The events list on each homepage pulls from a Google Sheet — in demo mode it shows sample data. When Bev is ready, she adds a row to a spreadsheet and it appears on the site automatically. No login. No webmaster. No waiting.
