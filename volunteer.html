Volunteer

This park runs on people who show up. No budget, no staff army — just a community that decided this place was worth taking care of. Come be part of that.

Many of our volunteers come weekly on specific days. Others drop in whenever they can. We'll work with whatever you've got. You don't need to know anything about plants to start — you just need to show up.

Thursday Morning Workdays

Our Thursday workdays are the heartbeat of the park. High school students, retirees, longtime Bradenton residents, people who just moved here — all showing up together from 9am to noon. We weed, propagate, plant, identify, maintain, and occasionally argue about whether a vine is native. It's genuinely fun.

Bring gloves if you have them. We'll supply tools, water, snacks, and fresh air. 9am–Noon · All ability levels welcome.

Ways to help

🌱

Gardening & Nursery

Planting, propagating cuttings, weeding, trimming, yard waste cleanup. The core of what keeps the park looking the way it does.

🔨

Handyman Maintenance

Painting, light carpentry, pressure washing, general repairs. If you have a useful skill, we have a use for it.

📱

iNaturalist & Documentation

Log plant and wildlife observations using iNaturalist. Help us build the scientific record of what lives here. Every confirmed sighting matters.

🎪

Community Outreach

Represent the park at the Farmer's Market, Chamber events, the County Fair. If you like talking to people about something you love, this is your job.

📋

Office Support

Mailers, filing, data entry, thank you cards, graphic design. Remote-friendly options available.

🎓

Education & Tours

Lead classes, workshops, field trips, and guided tours. Help us share what this place knows.

🌺

Plant Sales

Spring and Fall plant sales (typically March and October). Setup, sales floor, breakdown. Great energy, good crowds.

🎄

Winter Nights Under the Lights

Our biggest annual event each December. All hands on deck — and it's genuinely magical. Over 8,500 visitors last year.
