Rent the Park

Weddings, parties, corporate events, and private gatherings in one of Manatee County's most beautiful outdoor settings. Venue rentals are how we keep the park free for the public — your event directly supports the mission.

The Setting

Ten acres of lush botanical gardens, three lakes, a butterfly garden, rare palms, and subtropical plantings — all private for your event. The Galleria building and Baden building (office/Bride's Room) are included in rentals.

The park's protected microclimate makes it a year-round venue. Rainfall and temperature here tend to be gentler than surrounding areas — a legacy of the park's former life as the county nursery.

What's Included

  • Exclusive use of the full 10-acre park for your event
  • Galleria building (indoor/outdoor event space)
  • Baden building — office and Bride's/VIP Room
  • Tables, chairs, and cocktail tables (recently upgraded via Selby Foundation grant)
  • On-site manager from one hour before start until all guests depart
  • Parking
