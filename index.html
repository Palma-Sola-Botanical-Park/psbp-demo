"We are not Selby Gardens. We are something else entirely — a community that decided a piece of land was worth fighting for, and never stopped."

In 1990, neighbors rallied to save a surplus county nursery. They didn't have a plan. They had conviction. Thirty-five years later, Palma Sola Botanical Park is Manatee County's only botanical park — 10 frost-free acres packed with rare palms, exotic fruits, and subtropical plants you won't find anywhere else in the region.

We run on volunteer power and community donations. No government subsidy. No entrance fee, ever. Just people who love this place keeping it alive for everyone.

Get Your Hands Dirty

High school workdays. Thursday mornings. All welcome.

We pull weeds. We propagate cuttings. We identify plants we've never noticed before. Sometimes someone brings kolaches. Bill usually knows what that vine is. Sometimes he's wrong and we all learn together.

You don't need to know anything about plants. You need to show up. That's the whole job.
